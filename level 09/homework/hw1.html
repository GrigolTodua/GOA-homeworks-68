About Whales

Whales are large marine mammals that live in oceans all around the world. They are known for their intelligence, long migrations, and complex songs.

Types of Whales

  • Blue Whale
  • Humpback Whale
  • Beluga Whale
  • Orca (Killer Whale)

Conservation

Many species of whales are endangered due to hunting, pollution, and climate change. Conservation efforts focus on protecting their habitats and preventing illegal hunting.
